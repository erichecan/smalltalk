From your conversations

Joy

A feeling of great pleasure and happiness.

"Her eyes sparkled with joy."

Compliment

To express admiration or approval.

"He paid her a lovely compliment on her dress."

Cheer up

To make someone feel less sad or disappointed.

"I tried to cheer him up after he lost the game."

Get along

To have a friendly relationship with someone.

"Do you and your sister get along?"

Struggle

To be in a difficult situation.

"She struggled to understand the complex theory."

Astonished

To be very surprised or shocked.

"I was astonished by the news."

Thrilled

To be very happy and excited.

"She was thrilled with her exam results."
