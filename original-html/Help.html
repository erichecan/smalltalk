Common Questions

How do I start a new conversation? expand_more

Simply tap the 'New Conversation' button on the home screen and choose a topic or scenario to begin practicing your English skills!

Can I change my account password? expand_more

Yes, you can change your password in the 'Account Settings' section of your profile. Look for the 'Change Password' option and follow the instructions.

What if the app is not responding? expand_more

Try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, check your internet connection or restart your device. For more detailed steps, please visit the 'Troubleshooting' category or contact support.
